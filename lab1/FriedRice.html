Fried Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked rice, preferably cold or day-old
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots, thawed
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil and sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the peas and carrots and cook for 2 minutes more.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and add the eggs. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  5. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, fish sauce, salt, and pepper. Stir-fry until the rice is heated through and evenly coated with the sauce.
  6. Serve immediately.

Tips:

    For best results, use cold or day-old rice. Freshly cooked rice will be too wet and sticky.

    You can use any type of cooked rice, but long-grain rice, such as jasmine or basmati, will give you the best results.

    Use a variety of vegetables in your fried rice. I like to use onions, carrots, peas, and mushrooms.

    You can add any type of protein to your fried rice, such as chicken, shrimp, or beef.

    Be sure to stir-fry the rice until it is heated through and evenly coated with the sauce.

    Serve your fried rice with your favorite toppings, such as soy sauce, hot sauce, or chopped scallions.
